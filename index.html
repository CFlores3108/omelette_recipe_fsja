Omelette Recipe

Classic Omelette

Prep

5 mins

Cook

7 mins

Total

12 mins

Ingredients

2-3 large eggs

room temperature

1/4 cup whole milk

1/4 teaspoon kosher salt

2 tablespoons usalted butter

Instructions

1. Crack 2-3 eggs into a bowl.

2. Add 1-2 tablespoons of milk.

3. Add a pinch of salt.

4. Whisk eggs, milk, and salt together.

5. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.

6. Add a small amount of butter or oil.

7. Pour egg mixture into the pan.

8. Cook until edges start to set.

9. Add fillings like cheese, ham, or veggies.

10. Fold the omelette in half.

11. Cook for another 1-2 minutes.

12. Slide onto a plate and serve.
